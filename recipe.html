Banana and Strawberry Jam

This healthy jam is quick and easy to make, a hit with the kids,
and you can cook it while you brew the coffee and mix the pancake dough!

PREP: 1 minute COOK: Less than 10 minutes YELD: 3 or less
INGREDIENTS*
  • 2 cups of sliced strawberries
  • 1 chopped banana
  • 1 spoon of sugar
  • A dash of salt

* Click the items to check/uncheck them

DIRECTIONS*

1. Put the pan on the stove top, on high heat;

2. Put the strawberry, the chopped bananas, salt and sugar in it;

3. Stir occasionally when starts boiling;

4. After the liquid is partially reduced, turn off the fire;

5. Wait to cool and serve on top of bread, pancake, crepe etc.
